Hi, I'm Fauzan Afryan, a Junior Frontend Developer & Web Designer 17 years old from Indonesia, with a strong passion for crafting digital interfaces that are beautiful, intuitive, and memorable for every user.

My interest in technology and programming has been growing since I was young, long before I truly understood how it all worked. That curiosity is what eventually pushed me to start learning seriously, and ever since then I have never stopped. I am currently studying at SMK Negeri 1 Sumbawa Besar. where I continue to develop my technical foundation and creativity in the world of software development.

With over a year of hands-on experience, I am used to working on personal projects independently, from designing the visual side all the way to bringing it to life as a real, usable product. That process is what I enjoy the most: turning an idea in my head into something people can actually see, feel, and interact with, usually over a cup of coffee late into the dark night.

Outside of coding, I am someone who always keeps up with the latest developments in technology with genuine enthusiasm. I believe the best innovations are born from curiosity that never fades, and I always strive to understand not only how a technology works, but also why it was designed that way in the first place.

I am driven by creativity, attention to detail, and a constant desire to grow. One day, I want to be part of the generation that does not just use technology but actively shapes its direction and face for millions of people.
